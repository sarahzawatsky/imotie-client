emotions can be pesky. they can creep up on us at inconvenient times making it harder for us to focus, learn, and make friends. sometimes our emotions can even cause us to get in trouble! uh-oh!

but emotions can also be powerful! they can *help* us focus and make us *more* empathetic, allowing us to be better friends to others and most importantly, be better friends to ourselves.

when emotions creep up on us, they can quickly overwhelm us and before we know it, we may feel out of control! learning to identify what out emotions look like, what causes them, and how intensely we feel them can help us stay in control of our minds and bodies, ultimately helping us to feel happier and more comfortable in our environments.

so go ahead and give imotie a whirl!
